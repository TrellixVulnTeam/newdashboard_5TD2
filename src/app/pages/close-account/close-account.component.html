Close Account

Step 1 | Cancel or transfer your existing services and domains

The first thing you should focus on is canceling or transferring your existing services, for example, any Premium plans, domains, mailboxes, and app subscriptions that you have connected to your account. These should all be canceled or transferred before you close your account.

Step 2 | Move your sites to the trash

Next, move any remaining sites in your account to the trash. This ensures all your sites are deleted correctly.

Step 3 | Remove any domains in your account

If you have not yet canceled or transferred your domains as part of step 1 above, you need to do step now before you can proceed to the final step. Domains: If you did not cancel your domain or transfer it together with your site in step one, you have two options:
Transfer your domain to another account.
Transfer your domain to another domain host.
Connected Domain: If your domain is hosted by another domain host and connected to your site, remove it from your account.

Step 4 | Close your account

Once you've moved your remaining sites to the trash, canceled all existing premium services and domains, you can close your account.
